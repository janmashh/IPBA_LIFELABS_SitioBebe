Sitio Bebe

As we all know, COVID-19 or the Pandemic has seriously affected the lives of many.
People are losing their loved ones as they die due to COVID-19, Parents working at home to gain the money they need for their children,
Homeless people not knowing where they can go due to the fact that they have no shelter nor money to buy shelter and more of their needs.

Though a lot of people are helping in Quezon City, Pasig, Baguio, Cebu and so on, what about a place in Pampanga?
Have you heard of the Sitio Bebe Barangay? Let’s talk about what is happening in Sitio Bebe.

About Sitio Bebe

Sitio Bebe is located in Pampanga and right now they are having some major difficulties due to obviously the Pandemic or COVID-19.
Though why are they having a hard time? Let’s dig in more information.

Kuya Tami from Brgy. Sitio Bebe, He has to gather water from the well and boil the water for he or the people in Sitio Bebe to drink clean water.

Though the people in Sitio Bebe are still having a hard time due to the fact that they are also going through a severe health crisis.
Sitio Bebe isn’t close to a natural waterway or canal, the (local) government often imports water to the people in Sitio Bebe or send them to water wells,
though because of this, drought often happens especially in the summer.

Most of the people in Sitio Bebe work either in the factory or in caves to mine which has a disadvantage, they lack medical help
or Sitio Bebe barely has health professionals (e.g. Doctor/s, Nurses & etc.), so most of the people in Sitio Bebe get sick
which is sad to see. Their situation gets even worse due to COVID-19 and this is why we, Main IV, are here to help.
